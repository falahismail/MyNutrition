About Us

My Nutrition By Falah is a nutrition consulting company that provides nutrition services to clients in the Greater Toronto Area. We provide services such as nutrition counselling, meal planning, and nutrition education. Our goal is to help clients achieve their nutrition goals and live a healthy lifestyle.

Nutrition Counselling

Our nutrition counselling service is a one-on-one session with a Registered Dietitian. During the session, the dietitian will assess your current diet and provide recommendations on how to improve your diet. The dietitian will also provide you with a personalized meal plan to help you achieve your nutrition goals.

Meal Planning

Our meal planning service is a personalized meal plan created by a Registered Dietitian. The meal plan will be tailored to your nutrition goals and dietary needs. The meal plan will include a list of foods to eat and a list of foods to avoid. The meal plan will also include a list of recipes to help you prepare your meals.

Nutrition Education

Our nutrition education service is a one-on-one session with a Registered Dietitian. During the session, the dietitian will provide you with information on nutrition and how to improve your diet. The dietitian will also provide you with a personalized meal plan to help you achieve your nutrition goals.
